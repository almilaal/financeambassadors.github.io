About Finance Ambassadors

Finance Ambassadors is an international eTwinning project designed to raise financial awareness among students and help them develop essential money management skills for everyday life. Financial literacy today is not only about earning money, but also about understanding how to manage, save, invest, and spend responsibly.

Students learn how to create personal budgets, distinguish between needs and wants, develop saving strategies, and become conscious consumers. Through activities such as poster design, budgeting exercises, discussions, and presentations, they gain practical life skills and real-world financial awareness.

A key strength of the project is its international collaboration.
